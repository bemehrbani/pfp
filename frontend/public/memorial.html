Who Fired the Missiles?

An open-source intelligence investigation traced the origin of fire

  • 🎯BGM-109 Tomahawk cruise missile — a U.S.-only weapon system
  • 🚢Launched from USS Abraham Lincoln Carrier Strike Group, Arabian Sea
  • 📏~300-400 km south of Minab — well within Tomahawk's 1,600 km range
  • 🏫School mistargeted using outdated 2013 intelligence — it had been a civilian school since 2016
  • 📄U.S. DoD preliminary assessment acknowledged "likely" responsibility

Sources: Al Jazeera, Human Rights Watch, NYT, CBC, NPR, Bellingcat, Planet Labs satellite imagery

Peer Organizations & Justice Ecosystem

Organizations for partnership, coalition, and learning · Focus on The Hague / Netherlands

Tier 1 — Direct Peers

Organizations directly aligned with international justice and accountability work.

Global Rights Compliance (GRC)

Stichting · The Hague Est. 2013 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

International law foundation promoting accountability and strengthening justice systems. Provides specialized legal expertise in conflict-affected regions.

Action: Study their governance model; reach out for mentorship and partnership after KVK registration.

TRIAL International

NGO · Geneva (global ops) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Fighting impunity for international crimes — genocide, crimes against humanity, war crimes. Provides legal assistance, litigates cases, develops local capacities.

Action: Study their victim support model; explore case collaboration on evidence-gathering.

Justice & Peace Netherlands

Stichting · The Hague ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Human rights protection, peacebuilding, and advocacy. Established Dutch peer with strong advocacy model.

Action: Contact for coalition-building and potential board advisor recommendations.

Tier 2 — Coalition Partners

Coalition for the ICC (CICC)

Coalition · The Hague ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Civil society coordination for ICC engagement. Gateway to ASP observer status and ICC engagement.

Action: Apply for membership immediately after KVK registration. Key pipeline to ASP participation.

PAX

Stichting · Utrecht / The Hague ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Largest Dutch peace organization. Dedicated to protecting civilians, ending armed conflict, and building inclusive peace.

Action: Join advocacy network; attend public events for visibility and NL government connections.

International Justice Mission (IJM) Netherlands

Stichting · The Hague ⭐⭐⭐

Combating slavery, trafficking, and violence. Different focus but same legal structure and fundraising model.

Action: Study their ANBI compliance model and Dutch fundraising approach.

Tier 3 — Ecosystem & Infrastructure

The Hague Humanity Hub

Co-working Hub · The Hague ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Houses 50+ peace and justice organizations. Physical presence, networking, credibility.

Action: Apply for membership / desk space when operational.

The Hague International Centre

Municipal Support · The Hague ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Helps international organizations settle in The Hague. Practical support for registration, networking.

Action: Contact for newcomer support and introductions to ecosystem.

Note: The Hague Justice Portal

Defunct: The Hague Justice Portal (launched 2006) was closed in ~2013 by The Hague Academic Coalition due to funding reallocation. It is no longer an active organization.

OKR updated to target alternative engagement paths:

  • Coalition for the ICC — active, directly relevant
  • Humanity Hub — physical presence in justice ecosystem
  • Hague International Centre — practical newcomer support

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1
Draft Association Rules (Säännöt)
📝 Mahdi + Zahra Required in Finnish

The rules (säännöt) must include all of the following:

  • Name: People for Peace & Justice ry
  • Domicile: Helsinki (Kotipaikka: Helsinki)
  • Purpose: Non-profit legal research and advocacy for justice
  • Membership conditions: How to join, fees (if any)
  • Board composition: Chairperson + minimum 2 members
  • Accounting period: Calendar year (Jan 1 – Dec 31)
  • Meeting procedures: Annual meeting timing, notice period
  • Dissolution clause: Assets go to a similar non-profit purpose
Key: Rules must be in Finnish or Swedish. Draft in English first, then get professional translation to Finnish (~€100–€150). PRH provides a model rules template you can adapt.
Step 2
Confirm 3 Founders & Board
👥 Zahra + Mahdi Minimum 3 founders
  • Founder 1 (Chairperson): Mahdi Farimani — Helsinki, Finland ✓
  • Founder 2: Zahra Azhar — Netherlands (board member)
  • Founder 3: TBD — need one more person (any nationality)

Each founder needs: full name, date of birth, nationality, and address. No passport copies needed for registration — just data.

Note: Foreigners can be founders and board members. Only the chairperson must reside in Finland.
Step 3
Hold Founding Meeting
📋 All 3 founders Can be virtual

The founding meeting must:

  • Adopt the association rules (säännöt)
  • Elect the board of directors (chairperson + 2 members)
  • Sign the founding charter (perustamiskirja)
  • Approve the PRH registration application
  • Document everything in minutes (pöytäkirja)
Founding charter must be signed by all founders. If meeting is virtual, use electronic signatures or send physical documents for ink signatures.
Step 4
Register with PRH
🇫🇮 Mahdi €55 · ~2 days
  • File online at prh.fi (requires Finnish bank ID / mobile certificate)
  • Upload: rules, founding charter, board details, minutes
  • Pay €55 registration fee online
  • PRH reviews and adds to Register of Associations
  • Receive association register extract = your proof of existence
ID requirement: Online filing requires Finnish personal identity code + Finnish e-ID (bank credentials). Mahdi handles this as the Finnish-resident chairperson.
Step 5
Open Bank Account
🏦 Mahdi 1–2 weeks
  • Visit Finnish bank (OP, Nordea, or S-Pankki) with registration extract
  • Documents: association register extract, board minutes, chairperson's ID
  • Association needs its own separate account — personal accounts won't work
  • Set up online banking for the association
Step 6
Apply for Tax-Exempt Status
📊 Mahdi Vero (Tax Admin)

Apply to Finnish Tax Administration (Vero) for yleishyödyllinen yhteisö (public-benefit entity) status:

  • Operates exclusively for the public good
  • Activities benefit the broader public (not a closed group)
  • No financial benefit to members or board

Tax-exempt income includes: donations, grants, membership fees, dividends. Business income taxed at 20%.

Fit: An NGO focused on legal research and human rights documentation clearly meets public-benefit criteria.
Step 7
Launch Operations
🚀 Mahdi + Zahra Ongoing
  • Publish association info on campaign website
  • Set up bookkeeping (simple cash accounting OK for small associations)
  • Begin membership recruitment
  • Apply for MFA / KIOS Foundation grants
  • Contact Coalition for the ICC about membership
  • Prepare for ASP 25 observer accreditation (deadline ~Sep 2026)

Pre-Registration Checklist

Before the Founding Meeting
  • Rules (säännöt) drafted in English and translated to Finnish
  • Association name confirmed unique (check at prh.fi)
  • 3 founders identified with full details (name, DOB, nationality, address)
  • Chairperson confirmed as Finnish resident (Mahdi ✓)
  • Board composition agreed (chairperson + 2 members minimum)
  • Accounting period decided (recommend calendar year)
At the Founding Meeting
  • Rules adopted by all founders
  • Board elected: chairperson, secretary, treasurer (or combined)
  • Founding charter (perustamiskirja) signed by all founders
  • Minutes recorded (pöytäkirja)
  • Decision to register with PRH approved
After Registration
  • Receive PRH registration extract
  • Open association bank account
  • File for tax-exempt status with Vero
  • Publish association info on website
  • Set up simple bookkeeping
  • Apply for CICC membership

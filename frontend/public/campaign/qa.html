🧪 QA Test Script

Follow each test case in order. Record pass/fail, notes, and screenshots. Estimated time: ~30 minutes.

🤖 Open Bot ⏱ ~30 min 📋 8 test cases 📅 Last updated: Mar 15, 2026

ℹ️ Context for the Tester

  • This bot helps volunteers support the Justice for Minab Children campaign
  • 168 children were killed in a school attack — we're building awareness and demanding accountability
  • The bot gives volunteers small tasks (2-5 min each): tweet, retweet, sign petition, create art
  • Our target audience: Iranian diaspora, international activists, artists
  • Many users are Telegram-native (no active Twitter) — that's why we added the Telegram forwarding path
  • Biggest challenge: making the flow clear enough that a first-time volunteer can complete a task without asking anyone for help
TC1 First Contact & Onboarding
StepActionExpected
1.1Open this linkBot opens in Telegram
1.2Tap StartBot sends welcome message
1.3Read the welcome messageShould explain: what happened, what we're doing, how you help
1.4Enter your name when askedBot confirms name, shows campaign card
1.5Tap Join on the campaign cardAuto-join, show campaign info with resources

Key Questions

  • Did you understand the cause within 10 seconds?
  • Did you know what to do next?
  • Was anything confusing or unclear?
TC2 Task List & Priority
StepActionExpected
2.1View the task list after joiningTasks sorted easiest first
2.2Check the first taskShould have ⭐ badge — "Start Here" task
2.3Read task namesClear, not truncated mid-word
2.4Check time/points displayEach task shows ⏱ X min · Y pts

Key Questions

  • Is it obvious which task to start with?
  • Do the task names make sense without clicking into them?
  • Does the list feel overwhelming or manageable?
TC3 Sign the Petition (Easiest Task)
StepActionExpected
3.1Tap the petition taskShow instructions + petition link
3.2Open the petition linkGoes to ECCHR petition page
3.3Sign the petitionPetition form should work
3.4Come back to botBot should be waiting for proof
3.5Send proof (screenshot or URL)Bot confirms + offers next task

Key Questions

  • Was it clear what "proof" means?
  • Did the bot guide you through it?
  • How long did the whole thing take?
TC4 Amplify Reports (Dual-Path)
StepActionExpected
4.1Start the Amplify taskShows Option A (Twitter) AND Option B (Telegram)
4.2Path A: Tap "Find Tweets"Opens Twitter search with hashtags
4.3Path B: Tap "Get Forwardable Message"Bot sends clean, forwardable message
4.4Try forwarding that messageForwards cleanly (no broken formatting)
4.5Send proof (screenshot or "done")Bot should accept it

Key Questions

  • Was the dual-path choice clear?
  • Did the forwardable message look good when forwarded?
  • Would you actually forward it to a real group?
TC5 Share a Child's Story (Twitter Post)
StepActionExpected
5.1Start the "Share a Child's Story" taskShows child picker or story options
5.2Pick a childBot sends photo + story + ready tweet
5.3Tap tweet intent linkOpens Twitter with pre-filled tweet
5.4Come back and paste tweet URLBot should confirm

Key Questions

  • Was the child's story emotionally impactful?
  • Did the tweet link work correctly?
  • Would you actually post this on your real Twitter?
TC6 Content Creation (Artist Task)

Phase 1

Artist Outreach Network

Week 1-2 · March 15-28

Leverage our database of 174 Iranian artists for the "Art for Peace" viral loop. Artists create → submit via bot → gallery & channel → volunteers share → new visitors → more artists.

  • Prepare personalized email template for 20 priority artists
  • Send Phase 1 emails (20 artists with personal connection)
  • Prepare bulk email for remaining 154 artists
  • Send bulk outreach after Phase 1 response analysis
  • Build gallery.html page for submitted artworks
  • Create submission flow in bot
Phase 2

Telegram Group Seeding

Week 1-2 · March 15-28

Identify and join 50+ Iranian diaspora, human rights, and activist Telegram groups. Share campaign briefings and bot deep links using the volunteer briefing templates.

  • Map top 50 Telegram groups (Iranian diaspora, human rights, activism)
  • Prepare group-specific introduction messages
  • Send briefings to top 10 most active groups
  • Monitor join rates and optimize messaging
  • Expand to remaining 40 groups
Phase 3

March 28 Twitter Storm

March 28 (Deadline Day)

Coordinated Twitter storm on the 1-month mark of the Minab school attack. Goal: trend #JusticeForMinabChildren globally. Volunteers use the bot to get pre-written tweets and post simultaneously.

  • Create Twitter Storm briefing message for channel
  • Prepare 10-15 ready-to-post tweets with statistics
  • Schedule 3 Telegram reminders (1 week, 1 day, 1 hour before)
  • Coordinate posting window (peak US+EU timezone: 2PM UTC)
  • Track hashtag performance in real-time
  • Post results celebration message to channel
Phase 4

Micro-Influencer Outreach

Week 3-4 · March 29 - April 15

After the Twitter Storm creates momentum, leverage the results to DM micro-influencers (1K-100K followers) in human rights and Iranian diaspora spaces. Show them the movement is real and growing.

  • Compile list of 50 micro-influencers on Twitter/Instagram
  • Prepare personalized DM templates with campaign metrics
  • Send outreach in batches of 10
  • Follow up with non-responders after 3 days
  • Convert interested influencers to campaign ambassadors

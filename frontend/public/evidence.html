Verified facts, OSINT findings, and media coverage documenting the Minab School airstrike of February 28, 2026.

⏱ Timeline of Events

Feb 28, 2026 — ~10:45 AM IRST
First Strike Hits Shajareh Tayyebeh School
Three Tomahawk cruise missiles strike the school compound in Minab, Hormozgan province during morning classes. The compound housed approximately 400 students aged 7-12.
Feb 28, 2026 — Afternoon
First Responders Arrive
Iranian Red Crescent and local emergency teams begin rescue operations. Initial casualty reports emerge. Social media videos show devastation at the site.
March 1-3, 2026
International Media Coverage Begins
Al Jazeera, BBC, Middle East Eye publish initial reports. Death toll confirmed at 165+. OSINT investigators begin analyzing weapon fragments.
March 5-10, 2026
OSINT Investigations Confirm US Weaponry
Independent analysts confirm BGM-109 Tomahawk fragments. Satellite imagery shows the school was separated from adjacent military site for 10+ years.

🔍 OSINT Findings

🚀 Weapon Identification

Video footage from Mehr News Agency shows cruise missile consistent with BGM-109 Tomahawk TLAM. Independently confirmed by Bellingcat and weapons experts based on size, shape, and explosion pattern. Tomahawk is a US-exclusive weapon — Israel does not possess it.

OSINT Verified

📡 Launch Platform

US Navy vessel (Arleigh Burke-class destroyer or Ticonderoga-class cruiser) from the USS Abraham Lincoln (CVN-72) carrier strike group. Launched from Arabian Sea / Gulf of Oman, approximately 300-400 km south of Minab.

OSINT Verified

🗺️ Regional Force Allocation

Strike occurred during "Operation Epic Fury" (Feb 28, 2026). US forces assigned to southern Iran (Hormozgan), Israeli forces to northern Iran. DoD map confirms Minab within US strike zone.

🛰️ Satellite Evidence — Civilian School

2013 imagery shows building as IRGC Naval compound. 2016-2026 imagery (Google Maps + Planet Labs) confirms it was walled off from military site for 10+ years, with separate entrance and playground equipment — functioning as a civilian school. Attack based on outdated intelligence.

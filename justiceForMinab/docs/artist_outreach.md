## 📧 Personalized Email (First 20 — Iranian artists + Selected artists)

**Subject**: Your art for peace is needed now — 168 children of Minab

---

Hi {Artist Name},

You submitted "{Submission Name}" to the Art for Life exhibition — a piece about peace, about healing.

Since then, 168 children were killed in the Minab school attack in Iran. Their names are known. Their faces are remembered.

We built a memorial for them: https://peopleforpeace.live/memorial.html

Now we need artists like you to turn grief into action. Here's what you can do **in 2 minutes**:

1. **Join the campaign**: https://t.me/peopleforpeacebot?start=campaign_18
2. Pick a task — retweet a report, share a child's story, or create new art
3. That's it. Every action is tracked and counts.

If you want to create something new — an illustration, a poem, a video, anything — we'll share it with our growing network and credit you.

The one-month anniversary is March 28. We're building toward a coordinated Twitter storm that day. Your voice matters.

With hope,
Mahdi Farimani
People for Peace

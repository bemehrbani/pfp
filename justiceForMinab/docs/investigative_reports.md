## 🟠 Tier 2 — Investigative Analysis & Legal Reports

### 8. ProPublica — Civilian Harm Mitigation Failures
- **URL**: [propublica.org — How the Pentagon dismantled its civilian harm prevention program before Minab](https://www.propublica.org/article/pentagon-civilian-harm-mitigation-dismantled-minab)
- **Published**: March 2026
- **Key findings**: Connected Minab strike to Trump administration's dissolution of Civilian Harm Mitigation and Response (CHMR) program; Secretary Hegseth prioritized "lethality" over protection; former officials said CHMR could have prevented tragedy; "more aggression, less accountability"

### 9. New Lines Institute — Legal Analysis
- **URL**: [newlinesinstitute.org — The Legal Implications of Targeting a Girls' Elementary School in Iran](https://newlinesinstitute.org/iran/the-legal-implications-of-targeting-a-girls-elementary-school-in-iran/)

- **Key findings**: Strike violated principles of distinction, proportionality, and military necessity under IHL; children entitled to special protection; publicly available information could have established the school's civilian character — a failure to distinguish civilians from combatants

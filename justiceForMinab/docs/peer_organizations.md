# Peer Organizations & Justice Ecosystem Map

> **Purpose:** Map organizations for partnership, coalition, and learning  
> **Focus:** The Hague / Netherlands-based international justice NGOs  
> **Last updated:** March 15, 2026

---

## Tier 1 — Direct Peers (International Justice & Accountability)

### Global Rights Compliance (GRC)
- **Type:** Stichting (The Hague)
- **Founded:** 2013
- **Focus:** Accountability, international law, conflict zones
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Same legal domain, same city, model for our structure
- **Website:** globalrightscompliance.org
- **Action:** Study their structure; reach out for mentorship/partnership

### TRIAL International
- **Type:** NGO (HQ: Geneva, operations worldwide)
- **Focus:** Fighting impunity for international crimes — legal assistance, litigation, capacity building
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Directly aligned with evidence-gathering and ICC engagement
- **Website:** trialinternational.org
- **Action:** Study their victim support model; explore case collaboration

### Justice & Peace Netherlands
- **Type:** Stichting (The Hague)
- **Focus:** Human rights protection, peacebuilding, advocacy
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Established NL-based peer, strong advocacy model
- **Website:** justiceandpeace.nl
- **Action:** Contact for coalition-building, potential board advisor recommendations

---

## Tier 2 — Coalition Partners (Broader Peace & Justice)

### PAX
- **Type:** Stichting (Utrecht, The Hague advocacy office)
- **Focus:** Largest Dutch peace organization — civilian protection, conflict resolution
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Policy advocacy expertise, NL government relations
- **Website:** paxforpeace.nl
- **Action:** Join their advocacy network; attend public events for visibility

### Coalition for the ICC (CICC)
- **Type:** International coalition (The Hague)
- **Focus:** Civil society coordination for ICC engagement
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Gateway to ASP observer status and ICC engagement
- **Website:** coalitionfortheicc.org
- **Action:** Apply for membership; key pipeline to ASP participation

### International Justice Mission (IJM) Netherlands
- **Type:** Stichting (The Hague)
- **Focus:** Trafficking, slavery, violence against people in poverty
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐ — Different focus but same legal structure, fundraising model
- **Website:** ijmnl.org
- **Action:** Study their ANBI compliance model and Dutch fundraising approach

## Tier 3 — Ecosystem & Infrastructure

### The Hague Humanity Hub
- **Type:** Co-working / community hub
- **Focus:** Houses 50+ peace & justice organizations
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Physical presence, networking, credibility
- **Website:** humanityhub.org
- **Action:** Apply for membership/desk space when operational

### The Hague Institute for Global Justice
- **Type:** Independent non-profit
- **Focus:** Research on peace, security, and justice
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐ — Research collaboration, publications
- **Website:** thehagueinstituteforglobaljustice.org
- **Action:** Explore research partnerships

### The Hague International Centre
- **Type:** Municipal support organization
- **Focus:** Helping international organizations settle in The Hague
- **Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Practical support for registration, housing, networking
- **Website:** thehagueinternationalcentre.nl
- **Action:** Contact for newcomer support and introductions

## Important Note: The Hague Justice Portal

The original Hague Justice Portal (launched 2006) was **closed in ~2013** by The Hague Academic Coalition due to funding reallocation. It is no longer an active membership organization.

**Updated OKR recommendation:** Replace "Join The Hague Justice Portal" with:
- Join the **Coalition for the ICC** (active, relevant)
- Apply for **Humanity Hub** membership (physical presence)
- Register with **The Hague International Centre** (newcomer support)
